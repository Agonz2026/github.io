03

Track Strategy Detail

Track 1 · New Provider Outreach
Tactical Expansion
The Tech-Forward Value Hook
Lead with a "PIP Portfolio Audit" — show providers they may be leaving statutory interest uncollected. Use the firm's Python-based interest calculator as the demonstration tool. This positions G&A as a sophisticated financial partner, not just another law firm asking for referrals.

Collateral Required

"Recovery Roadmap" one-pager — file journey from provider office through Neos/Lawmatics to recovery
"First File Kit" — referral form, HIPAA release, intake instructions, business card
Brand welcome video — existing firm video, linked in Day 1 follow-up email

Mandatory 1-3-7 Follow-Up Sequence

Day 1
Thank You Email — digital business card + link to firm brand video
Day 3
First File Kit Drop-off — physical folder with everything needed to send the first case
Day 7
Phone Call — schedule a "Quick Audit" of one pending or denied bill to demonstrate PIP recovery process
21-Day Rule
If a new provider has not sent a case within 21 days of first visit, the engagement strategy for that office must shift. Escalate to an attorney-level introduction or pivot to a different contact within the practice.
Track 2 · Existing Provider Growth
Tiering Strategy

Provider Tiering Model

Tier Criteria Action Plan
Platinum 5+ cases / month (consistent) Quarterly Financial Recovery Report showing interest collected · Personal attorney touchpoint
Gold 1–3 cases / month Quarterly lunch · Quarterly check-in · "Lunch & Learn" for billing staff
Silver Dormant or inconsistent Monthly "Legal Update" newsletter · Periodic drop-in visits to re-verify contacts
Closed-Loop Feedback System
Providers stop referring when cases feel like a "black hole." Combat this with two automatic touchpoints: a Monthly Referral Report (Lawmatics — every PI case status) and a "Win" Success Alert sent immediately when a PIP suit settles. Celebrate recoveries to keep G&A top-of-mind.

Staff Training as a Retention Tool

Train front-desk staff to identify a Bodily Injury (PI) case vs. standard PIP intake — turns their staff into case scouts
Offer as a complimentary service — positions firm as "Compliance Partner," not just vendor
Host quarterly Zoom briefings (20 min) on Florida PIP statute changes affecting provider billing
